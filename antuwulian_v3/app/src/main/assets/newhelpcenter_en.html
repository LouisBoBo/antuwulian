Solution：Restar the device; log in to the monitoring platform (mobile APP, computer platform) to check the remaining power; charge the device, restart the device after it is full.

Solution：Cancel the setting of periodic positioning, the device will resume real-time positioning after the next cycle is online or after rebooting.

Solution：Contact our online customer service to recharge, re-start after recharging.

4.4 SIM card contact is not good.

Solution：Reinstall the SIM card in the direction of the card indicated on the card slot cover.

Solution：Leave the signal weak area.
